What is Boba?

Boba tea is a popular drink with a variety of flavors. You can choose a tea with milk, lemonade, or juice. The boba are actually < strong>pearls that we add to the drink for a bit of texture. The pearls can be made of tapioca, fruit gels, or small bits of real fruit. For more information, check out this video: What is Boba video.
